Frank Ocean

Frank Ocean is an innovative artist known for his unique blend of R&B and hip hop. His albums Channel Orange and Blonde are acclaimed for their deep lyrics and experimental sound. In addition to being an important voice for the LGBTQ+ community, he is admired for his authenticity and mysterious style.

Tyler, the Creator

Tyler, The Creator is an artist who blends rap, R&B, and jazz. Founder of Odd Future, he is known for albums like Flower Boy and IGOR, as well as his fashion brand, Golf Wang.

Accomplishments
  • Two Grammys
  • Fashion
  • Movies Soundtracks
  • 1# on the Billboard with IGOR

Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar is a highly influential rapper known for his deep lyrics and innovative albums like DAMN. and To Pimp a Butterfly. He’s won multiple Grammy Awards for his work.

Accomplishments
  • 13 Grammys
  • Pulitzer Prizer for Music
  • Cultural Impact
  • Recognition: Time 100 and BET
